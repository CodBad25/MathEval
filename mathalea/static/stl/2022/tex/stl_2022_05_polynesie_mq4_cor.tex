\begin{align*}
f(t) &= \dfrac{0,715}{2} \\
\iff -0,715 \e^{-t} + 0,715 &= \dfrac{0,715}{2} \\
\iff -0,715 \e^{-t} &= \dfrac{0,715}{2} - 0,715 \\
\iff -0,715 \e^{-t} &= -0,3575 \\
\iff \e^{-t} &= \dfrac{-0,3575}{-0,715} \\
\iff \e^{-t} &= 0,5 \\
\iff -t &= \ln(0,5) \\
\iff t &= -\ln(0,5) \\
\iff t &\approx 0,693
\end{align*}
 
$0,693 \times 60 = 41,58 $ et $0,58 \times 60 = 34,8$ soit 41 minutes et 35 secondes.
